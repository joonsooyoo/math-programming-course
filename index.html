Welcome to Introduction to Computer Science

This course provides an introduction to the fundamental concepts of computer science, including programming, data structures, algorithms, and computer systems.

Throughout the course, we will explore these concepts through a variety of programming assignments and projects, with an emphasis on practical problem-solving skills and software development best practices.

By the end of the course, you will have gained a solid foundation in computer science that will prepare you for further study or a career in the field.
